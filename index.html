Welcome to Antoniodemax Childrens Home

At Antoniodemax Children's Home, we believe in creating families. Our mission is to connect loving parents with children in need of a forever home, fostering a future forever filled with love, laughter, and endless joy possibilities. Join us on the journey of building a brighter future for every child, because every little one deserves a place to call home. You're all welcome to our humble aboard.

About Us

Our aim is measured by the countless families we've brought together and the lives we've transformed through adoption. Each of the successful match represents a new chapter of love, stability, and hope for children and parents alike.

Process

Step 1: Application

Begin the adoption process by applying the submission form in the Antoniodemax website. You can view available children and apply online by filling out the application form and providing the necessary personal information.

Step 2: Home Study

Undergo a home study evaluation conducted by a licensed social worker. This process assesses your readiness to adopt, ensuring that your home environment is safe and suitable for a child.

Step 3: Placement

Once approved, you'll meet the child, and finalize legal arrangements, with our expertise and begin the transition process. Receive support and guidance from adoption professionals every step of the way.
